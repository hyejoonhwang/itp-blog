User Scenario
- The user enters the virtual gym and first sees a short reminder of the space’s safety and behavior rules.
- Around each machine, floating signs show real-time equipment availability and queue status.
- The user looks around, decides which machine to use, and joins a queue.
- While waiting, the user moves to the yoga mat area to stretch or do light workouts like push-ups.
- When a notification appears saying it’s their turn, the user can either go to the selected machine or choose to skip and continue their current activity.
